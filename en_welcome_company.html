Company About

  • B2Link is dedicated to supporting the success of K-beauty brands beyond our border. We provide a new, unprecedented vision of 'Data-based Distribution'. Our goal is to be a global distribution platform, which leads the trade ecosystem with our partners.

    B2LiNK at large, has 3 different types of Business Models. Global Beauty PublisherProvision of services like distribution, marketing, PR, logistics, and finance to support the success of beauty brands overseas. B2B Platform InnovatorA B2B Platform that directly connects international retailers and brands with one another. Big Data Solution ProviderAnalyze the Big Data of China, Southeast Asia, and North America and use the analytics to provide solutions for distribution and marketing issues.

  • ONSIGHT, A Big beauty data service, collects and analyzes the distribution data of beauty brands overseas. We place great value in the continued growth of your brand and the maintenance of your brand ethos in the retail market. We provide Key Performance Indicator (KPI) essential to the protection of your brand ethos, and advise smart distribution based on data- driven decision making.

    ONSIGHT is a Data Service specializing in distribution. We provide basic indicators to help you understand the current distribution status of your brand, as well as analysis tools to help set strategic goals through comparative analysis of your competitors.

    K Beauty is creative and growing at a rapid speed, but many brands are struggling to obtain and manage information in overseas markets. ONSIGHT aims to be the third eye, through the usage of data, to set a clarified global direction for the K-beauty market. With B2Link’s ONSIGHT services, we are hopeful that the brands will gain a broader perspective of the market.

ONSIGHT Macro Service provides
an overview of all the beauty categories
distributed in major E-commerce stores across China.

The Macro Service analysis provides distribution flows and market trends in E-commerce stores across China.
Key insights from data can be derived for strategic product planning.

  • Monthly Data

    Dashboard

    Macro Dashboard provides monthly sales data of products under the Beauty Category distributed for E-Commerce across China. You will be able to track the number of products, sales volume and sale profit according to its channel. Additionally, information on the Top 10 sales by category, brand and store is provided by our service.

  • Understanding the Beauty Market Trend

    analysis of Beauty Category

    Data is provided for in-depth understanding of the current Beauty Category market. The sales share of each category is identified, and the top brands’ sales share and trend data by category is provided.

  • Popular Brand

    Brand distribution status

    The Macro Service analysis provides a comprehensive overview of the brand’s ranking in the market along with its market status using various types of data. We believe in developing a strategic business plan for the brand’s growth starting with a thorough understanding of the market changes.

  • Attribute Trends

    Main properties of cosmetic products

    We provide data on the attributes of cosmetics that are specifically distributed in E-commerce stores. By analyzing data on the number of products, sales volume, and sales profit, we help you plan out new products that reflect the market’s trend.

ONSIGHT Micro Service provides data
for individual product units by brand.

Through Micro Service, you will be able to keep tabs on data for brand distribution status by each channel, and individual product unit index of the brand.
We track key data for brands to execute smart distribution in major e-commerce stores across China,
and based on the aforementioned, we provide guidelines on how to identify and respond to distribution issues.

  • Key Data

    Board

    Micro Service Dashboard provides key data for specific brands that is simplified and easy to look at. In addition, it provides data for selling price, sales volume, brand’s highest sales store, and top selling products through a time series graph.

  • Comparison Analysis

    Weekly, Monthly Distribution Status

    We provide simplified data that compares your competitor’s key data with that of your own brand. The data will provide you with more insight on Sales profit, Sales share, and top selling products among other key categories.

  • Key indicators

    Sales price & Sales volume

    We provide price trends by taking into account data on sales price including tax, promotion information, and coupon information. We also provide sales trend by channel for each respective brand.

  • Consumer Feedback

    Analysis of current reviews

    We provide data on individual products based on its running channel, analyze consumers’ reaction of the products, and deliver any visible review trends. Such analysis helps formulate product development and brand strategy.

ONSIGHT Report Service monitors
the brand’s baseline sales price
and shares its analysis report on a daily basis.

With the provision of ONSIGHT’s report, the brand will have a comprehensive overview of its product and can determine
if the product is being distributed within the appropriate price range. Through such analytics data, the brand will have a better understanding of the price status in China’s major E-commerce stores and set a strategic market price according to this data.

Report process

  • 01

    Daily Monitoring

    Targeting your brand’s top selling products,
    we collect data of sales prices and event details from China’s leading e-commerce channels on a daily basis.

  • 02

    Analysis

    Collected data is analyzed in units of channels and stores. Insights are deduced based on the analysis of such data

  • 03

    Delivery of Daily Report

    The analytics are sent to the responsible manager by e-mail everyday.
    Furthermore, the manager can also request
    a specific subject matter to be analyzed and sent should there be any that interests them.
